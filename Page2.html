The detriments of living in Haarlemmerhoutkwartier:

The housing situation:

Small good and bad details can be found about any area, but Haarlemmerhoutkwartier has one obvious one: housing. Haarlemerhoutkwartier is the second richest area of Haarlem, and the housing prices reflect that. Even if you decide that it is the right place for you, the prices of living there might make you reconsider.

Some statistics

On average, a house in Haarlemerhoutkwartier is worth €636,000, which is €317,000 more than the Dutch average . This can be attributed to the fact that the inhabitants of Haarlemmerhoutkwartier make more money than the inhabitants of any other region in Haarlem. Another detriment that is compounded with the high housing prices, is the low house availability. 96% of houses in Haarlem are already inhabited. So even if you decide that you want to move there, it will be very difficult to find a place to live, and if you do it will almost definitely be very expensive
