The decision

Pros:

Haarlemerhoutkwartier is a beautiful district, and hopefully the pictures have shown you that. There are plenty of forests and parks and healthy areas to enjoy time outdoors alone, or with family or friends. The wonderful part is that you have access to all of that while still being in the city.

Cons:

At the same time, there is a price tag on all of this, and a very large price tag at that. Haarlemerhoutkwartier will be unaffordable for most people. And the benefits of the area are not limited to just its inhabitants; you could live in another district and take a short walk to use the same amenities.

Conclusion:

The Haarlemmerhoutkwartier area is very beautiful and an attractive place to move to. There are large and healthy forests and parks that are integrated into this part of the city, but that is also reflected in the cost and availability of housing. Overall, there are many pros and cons to moving here, and I hope this website made your decision a little easier!
